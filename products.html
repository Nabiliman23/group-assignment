Flavours

These are the available flavours of our products

Oranges

Fresh oranges are washed and their juice extracted using a juice extractor to make orange juice. The extracted juice is then filtered to eliminate any pulp or seeds before being blended with water and sweetener to achieve the ideal sweetness and tartness balance. After that, the juice is pasteurized to retain its freshness and kill any bacteria that may be present. The end result is a sweet and tangy orange juice with a bright and zesty flavor that is frequently liked for its refreshing and nutritional features.

Blackcurrant

Fresh blackcurrants are washed and the juice extracted using a juice extractor to make blackcurrant juice. The extracted juice is then mixed with water and sweetener to achieve the required sweetness/tartness balance. The juice is then filtered to remove any solids before being pasteurized to keep it fresh and kill any bacteria that may be present. The end result is a sweet and tangy blackcurrant juice with a distinct and fruity flavor that is widely consumed for its health advantages and refreshing taste.

Apple

Fresh apples are washed, peeled, and sliced into small pieces to prepare apple juice. After that, the pieces are crushed to extract the juice, which is then filtered to eliminate any solids such as pulp or seeds. The juice is then blended with water and sweetener to get the proper sweetness and tartness balance. After that, the juice is pasteurized to retain its freshness and kill any bacteria that may be present. The end result is a sweet and tangy apple juice with a bright and fruity flavor that is frequently liked for its refreshing and nutritional properties.

Manggo

Ripe mangoes are peeled, pitted, and pureed till smooth to make mango juice. The puree is then filtered to remove any fibrous material, and the resulting juice is blended with water and sugar to get the desired level of flavour and sweetness. After that, the juice is pasteurised to retain its freshness and kill any bacteria that may be present. The resulting mango juice is sweet and pleasant, with a rich tropical flavour.

lychee

Fresh lychees have their pits removed and then the fruit is pureed in a blender to generate lychee juice. Following this step, the puree is filtered to eliminate any particulates, and the resulting juice is combined with water and sweetener to get the required level of sweetness while maintaining the desired level of tartness. The finished product is a lychee juice that is fragrant, sweet, and has a flavour that can only be described as uniquely tropical.

Soy milk

Soybeans are first rehydrated and then mashed into a paste before being used to manufacture soy milk. After that, the paste is combined with water, and occasionally a sweetener and flavourings as well, and then it is boiled until the liquid is silky smooth and creamy. The resulting liquid is the soy milk, which is obtained by straining the mixture in order to eliminate any particles that may have been present. You can drink the soy milk on its own, or you can use it as a foundation for creating other beverages, such smoothies or lattes.

Chrysanthemum

Chrysanthemum tea is made by infusing dried chrysanthemum flowers in hot water to release its flavour and perfume. The tea is then filtered to remove the flowers and sweetened to taste with honey or sugar. The end result is a sweet and aromatic tea with a delicate floral flavour that is frequently liked for its health benefits and relaxing properties. Chrysanthemum tea is typically served hot, but it can also be served cold for a refreshing summer treat.

Cranberry and Apple

Fresh cranberries and apples are washed, peeled, and sliced into small pieces to prepare cranberry and apple juice. The pieces are then crushed to extract the juice, which is combined with water and sweetener to achieve the proper sweetness and tartness balance. The juice is then filtered to remove any solids before being pasteurised to keep it fresh and kill any bacteria that may be present. The result is a sweet and tangy cranberry and apple juice with a distinctive fruity flavour.

Cranberry and Pomegranate

Fresh cranberries and pomegranates are washed and their juice extracted to make cranberry and pomegranate juice. The juices are then combined with water and sweetener to achieve the ideal balance of acidity and sweetness. The juice is then filtered to remove any solids before being pasteurised to keep it fresh and kill any bacteria that may be present. The end result is a sweet and tangy cranberry and pomegranate juice with a distinct flavour profile that is frequently loved for its health advantages.

Blackcurrant and mixed berries

Blackcurrants and a variety of other berries, such as raspberries, blueberries, and strawberries, are cleaned and their juice extracted to form a blackcurrant and mixed fruit juice. The juices are then combined with water and sweetener to achieve the ideal balance of sweetness and tartness. The juice is then filtered to remove any solids before being pasteurised to keep it fresh and kill any bacteria that may be present. The end result is a tasty and juicy blackcurrant and mixed berry juice with a sweet and tart character.

Mixed fruit

Pomegranate, mango, and orange fruits are cleaned, peeled, and their juice is extracted to create a mixed fruit juice. The juices are then mixed with water and sweetener to achieve the ideal balance of sweetness and tartness. The juice is then filtered to remove any solids before being pasteurised to keep it fresh and kill any bacteria that may be present. The result is a sweet and pleasant mixed fruit juice with a distinct and delicious flavour profile that combines the tanginess of pomegranate, the richness of mangoes, and the sweetness of oranges.
